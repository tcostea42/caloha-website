What We Test
OBD-Eligible Vehicles
Under CARB's Clean Truck Check program, OBD testing is required for heavy-duty vehicles (over 14,000 lbs GVWR) with newer engines.
🚛

Diesel Vehicles

Heavy-duty diesel trucks and buses with 2013 or newer model year engines. Class 4–8 trucks, semis, box trucks, dump trucks, and diesel buses.

⛽

Alternative Fuel Vehicles

Natural gas (CNG/LNG) and other alternative fuel heavy-duty vehicles with 2018 or newer model year engines.

🔌

Hybrid Vehicles

Diesel-hybrid and alternative fuel hybrid heavy-duty vehicles meeting the above engine model year requirements.

🌾

Agricultural & Motorhomes

OBD-equipped on-road agricultural vehicles and CA-registered motorhomes over 14,000 lbs. Annual testing schedule.

🛣

Out-of-State Trucks

All heavy-duty vehicles operating on California roads must comply—regardless of registration state.

📋

Common Makes

Freightliner, Peterbilt, Kenworth, Volvo, Mack, International, Western Star, HINO, Isuzu, and more.

Note: We specialize in OBD testing only. Vehicles with 2012 or older diesel engines or 2017 or older alternative fuel engines require smoke opacity testing and visual inspections, which is a separate service.
